How It Works

Buy Meal:

  • List your VIP meal
    • Customize the VIP meal that you want in the app
  • Pay for the meal
    • Pay for the VIP meal in a secure environment created by Stripe
  • Wait for the meal to be ordered
    • Wait for the seller to find your VIP meal, order it on the Bama Dining app, and confirm that the VIP meal has been ordered in the app
  • Pick up your meal!

Sell Meal:

  • Find a VIP meal
    • Look for a meal that has been listed
  • Order the meal in the Bama Dining app
    • Order the meal exactly as listed in the order from the buyer
  • Confirm that the meal has been ordered
    • Confirm in the MealMatch app with a screenshot of the receipt
  • Get paid
    • When the 4-hour complaint period ends, funds will be released to you
